How do I get started with Arhatic Yoga Hub?

Download the app from the App Store, sign in with your Google account, and start creating your daily practice checklists. The app will guide you through the setup process.

How do I sync my data across devices?

Your data automatically syncs when you sign in with the same Google account on different devices. Make sure you're connected to the internet for the initial sync.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Google's secure authentication system and your data is encrypted and stored safely. We never share your personal information with third parties.

Can I export my journal entries?

Currently, journal entries are stored securely within the app. We're working on export features for future updates.

üêõ Troubleshooting

App won't open

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device and try again.

Can't sign in with Google

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and that you're using a valid Google account. Try signing out and back in if the issue continues.

Data not syncing

Check your internet connection and ensure you're signed in with the same Google account on all devices. Try pulling down to refresh the app.

App crashes

Update to the latest version of the app from the App Store. If the problem continues, please contact us with details about your device and iOS version.
